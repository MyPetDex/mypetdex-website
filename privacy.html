Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

The short version: Your data is 100% private. We never sell your personal information or your pet's information to anyone — not advertisers, not third parties, not anyone.

📋 Overview

MyPetDex ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the MyPetDex platform.

📥 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, including your name, email address, city, state, and pet information (such as breed, age, weight, vaccines, and reminders). We also collect usage data to improve the app experience.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or precise GPS location.

🔧 How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide our services — including displaying your pet profiles, sending reminders and notifications you have requested, and showing local service providers near you. We also use it to communicate important updates about your account.

🤝 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with essential service providers (such as Firebase for data storage and EmailJS for notifications) solely to operate the platform. We will never share your data with advertisers or marketing companies.

✅ Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at help@mypetdex.app. You can also delete your account directly from your account settings in the app.

🛡️ Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption, Firebase Authentication, Firestore Security Rules, and HTTPS to protect your data. Only you can access your personal information and pet records. No system is 100% secure, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

🍪 Cookies

We use essential cookies only to maintain your login session and app functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

👶 Children's Privacy

MyPetDex is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of MyPetDex after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

📬 Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at help@mypetdex.app.
